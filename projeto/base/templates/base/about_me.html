About Me

Hey there! My name is Tayla and my favorite thing in the world is telling stories through art! Right now I'm a freelancer based in Brazil with interests in working and studying abroad. I'm always experimenting with different forms of digital media; illustration, comics, animation, you name it! I like to use bold colors and lights to find new and interesting directions to art. I have a degree in architecture from Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, which helped me gain an understanding on how to compose a scene and convey depth through my linework. I've been in love with art, animation, and comics for as long as I can remember. If I can bring a bit of mischievous energy to what I do then I will, because if it makes me giggle it's worth doing!
